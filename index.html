Americano

Americano is a type of coffee beverage that is made by adding hot water to a shot or two of espresso. The resulting drink is similar in strength to drip coffee but has a different flavor profile due to the espresso. Americano is a popular coffee drink worldwide, especially in the United States and Europe.

Latte

Latte is a type of coffee beverage that is made with espresso and steamed milk. The term "latte" comes from the Italian word "caffe latte," which means "milk coffee."

Filter Coffee

Filter coffee is a type of coffee that is brewed by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans contained in a filter. The water slowly drips through the coffee and the filter, extracting the flavor and aroma of the beans, and collecting in a carafe or pot below.
